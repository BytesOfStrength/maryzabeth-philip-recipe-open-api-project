The Importance of Using Fresh Ingredients that are in Season

Using fresh in-season ingredients is amazing when it is available. I love shopping for local ingredients from a neighborhood store. Fruit, like strawberries, harvested at the peak of its season give amazing depth flavor to any minimally processed recipe.

Weather Matters: The impact of weather on your recipes.

As someone who bakes and works with chocolate and fresh ingredients, understanding how weather can effect your recipes is a must. The environment's temperature and humidity can make a beautiful whipped cream frosted cake look like cake soup. That's why I take into account the weather and check out the the current weather when I start a new project.

Check out our Weather Page to see the current conditions in Maryland and learn how they may affect your recipe process or even your choice of recipe.
